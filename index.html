Why Lansly

THINK AND SPEAK NO GRAMMAR TO BREAK

Developing four skills together in one learning process, receptive skills, reading and listening as well as productive skills, writing, and speaking.

Think

Most of the people who are going to learn English used to think in their mother tongue before they produce new sentences The obstacle of translation stays as a barrier in front of them With lansly we create a technique to allow the learner by time to express his/her ideas without translation to the mother tongue. Thus she/he is capable to think in English which is the key to become native

Speak

You speak English based on how many sentences are memorized in your mind or how many texts you have read. Using lansly you will be able to speak continuously without hesitation or carrying about making mistakes .because you have already trained to the brain on correct patterns and they will be produced automatically

No grammar to break

Fearness of making grammar mistakes through the learning process stop many people to continue their journey for mastering the English language lansly app considers this issue and suggest a set of strategies to overcome that fearness the learner can acquire the basic grammar rules effortlessly just by following the instructions that mentioned in the app
